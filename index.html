Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

A future-ready Bangladesh thrives on innovation, fostering a digital economy, research-driven industries, and a culture of entrepreneurship.

Sustainability

Economic growth must go hand in hand with environmental responsibility.Bangladesh 2.0 priority green energy and sustainable urban planning

Infrastructure

A strong foundation of world-class infrastructure, including smart cities, high-speed connectivity, and efficient transportation networks.

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 is about people-centric development,ensuring equal opportunities through education,skills development and social inclusion.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh has already shown the world what resilience, innovation and entrepreneurship can achieve. Now, as we move toward ‘Bangladesh 2.0,’ we must focus on building a future that is not only economically strong but also socially inclusive and environmentally sustainable.Bangladesh 2.0 is not just about GDP growth—it’s about human growth. A future where no one is left behind. A future where innovation serves humanity. Let us build that future together"

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
